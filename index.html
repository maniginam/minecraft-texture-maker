How to Make a Texture Pack

1
Draw your texture! Pick a color on the left and draw on the big grid in the middle. You can also click a block to start from that shape.
2
Pick what block it replaces Use the "This texture is for" dropdown to choose which Minecraft block or item your drawing will replace (like Dirt or Stone).
3
Click "Add to Pack" This saves your texture into your pack. You can make as many textures as you want! They all show up as little pictures in "Your Pack."
4
Name your pack Type a cool name in the "My Cool Pack" box. This is what it will be called in Minecraft!
5
Click "Download Pack!" This downloads a .zip file to your computer. Remember where it saves!
Now let's put it in Minecraft!
6
Open Minecraft Java Edition Go to the main menu and click Options...
7
Click "Resource Packs..." You'll see a screen with two columns. Your pack isn't there yet!
8
Click "Open Pack Folder" This opens a folder on your computer called resourcepacks.
9
Drag your .zip file into that folder Find the .zip file you downloaded and drag it right into the resourcepacks folder.
10
Go back to Minecraft and click your pack! Your pack should now appear on the left side. Click the arrow to move it to "Selected" on the right side. Click Done and your textures are in the game!
